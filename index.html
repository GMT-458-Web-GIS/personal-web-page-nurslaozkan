About Me

I am a Geomatics Engineering student at Hacettepe University, focusing on remote sensing, orbital mechanics, and geospatial analysis. I work on LiDAR data processing, GNSS-based positioning, spatial data interpretation, and satellite-driven mapping solutions. I actively use tools such as Python, ArcGIS Pro, QGIS, MicroStation, and Global Mapper while strengthening my skills in object-oriented programming and data processing.

My goal is to contribute to R&D projects that combine geospatial technologies with software development and produce innovative solutions in space and Earth observation systems. As someone committed to continuous technical growth and interdisciplinary collaboration, I aim to take part in projects where research, automation and engineering intersect. I am also eager to expand my professional network and collaborate within forward-looking technology-focused environments.
